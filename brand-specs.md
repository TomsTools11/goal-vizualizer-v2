# GOAL Brand Design Specifications

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue (GOAL Blue)**: #1E88E5 or similar bright blue
- **Dark Blue/Navy**: #1A365D (for text and dark elements)
- **Teal/Green**: #0D9488 (for positive metrics/percentages)
- **Gray**: #9CA3AF (for secondary/comparison bars)
- **Light Gray Background**: #F8FAFC
- **White**: #FFFFFF (card backgrounds)
- **Dark Text**: #1F2937

## Typography
- Bold headings for metric titles
- Large numeric displays for KPI values
- Clean sans-serif font family

## Component Patterns

### KPI Cards (Top Row)
- Large colored numbers (blue for currency, teal for percentages)
- Small gray label below
- Clean white card with subtle shadow

### Horizontal Bar Charts
- Primary item highlighted with GOAL blue bar
- Secondary/comparison items in gray
- Star icon (★) to mark primary/featured item
- Value displayed to the right of bar
- Card title in bold dark text

### Metric Types
1. Cost Per Lead (currency)
2. Cost Per Quote (currency)
3. Cost Per Policy (currency)
4. Quote → Policy Rate (percentage)
5. Spend by Campaign (currency)
6. Leads by Campaign (count)
7. Lead → Quote Conversion Rate (percentage)
8. Quote → Policy Conversion Rate (percentage)
9. Cost Per Click (currency, lower is better)
10. Cost Per Quote (currency, lower is better)
11. Contact Rate (percentage)
12. Lead → Sale (percentage)
13. Total Sales (count)

### Dashboard Layout
- Header with GOAL logo
- Summary badges (e.g., "Industry-Leading Performance")
- Highlight section with key multipliers (2.1x, 3.0x, etc.)
- Grid of metric cards (2 columns)
- Bottom line summary section

## Visual Style
- Clean, modern, professional
- Rounded corners on cards
- Subtle shadows
- Light background with white cards
- Emphasis on data clarity
